All knowledge is communally generated.
Here's how we can acknowledge it.

V1.0

The Relational Citation Style (RCS) recognizes that knowledge is generated and circulated through conversation, community, and networks of care—often instead of publication. RCS treats oral transmission and collaborative thinking as legitimate and valuable scholarly sources that should be properly credited and traced along its lineage.
